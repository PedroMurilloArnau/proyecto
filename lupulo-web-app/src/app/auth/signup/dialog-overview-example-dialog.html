Delivery

Joseph Holt deliver nationwide to the UK. Local deliveries within the Greater Manchester area are fulfilled and carried out by Joseph Holt. We work with a third party (WDS Group) to fulfil our national orders. Please note, all transactions on the Beer Shop are made through Joseph Holt Ltd. We do not share payment details with any third parties. We also operate a Click and Collect system on the Beer Shop, which is free of charge. During the checkout process, enter your postcode then select the Click and Collect option. You can currently click and collect from the Joseph Holt Brewery with two pick up slots available on Tuesdays and Fridays.

Delivery Charges

Standard delivery to local postcods is £4.99 per order. Standard delivery for national orders is £6.99. Click and Collect is a free service.

All orders over £50 (after any discounts/offers) are subject to free delivery and the postage fee will automatically be removed at the checkout. We reserve the right to remove free delivery on order over £50 at any time.

Failed Deliveries

We cannot accept responsibility for delivery failure if you provide an incorrect address, postcode or incorrect contact telephone number. Please check this information carefully when placing your order.
